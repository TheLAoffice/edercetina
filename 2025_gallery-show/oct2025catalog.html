The Artist

Eder Cetina is a Los Angeles–based artist whose work fuses urban abstraction, graffiti energy, and sacred geometry.

Through painting, installation, and architectural interventions, Cetina creates immersive experiences that bridge the grit of city textures with the transcendence of ritual.

The Exhibition

Urban Interventions transforms industrial space into a codex of color, rhythm, and memory.

This solo exhibition presents a compelling vision where street culture meets spiritual geometry, creating works that resonate with both contemporary collectors and institutional buyers.
